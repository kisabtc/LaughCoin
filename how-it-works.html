How LaughCoin Works

Getting LaughCoin (LAF): You can buy, trade, or earn LAF through various activities like community challenges or memes competitions.

Using LAF: Spend your LAF on unique NFTs, vote in community decisions, or stake your tokens for rewards.

Earning NFTs: Participate in our humor-based events to mint or win NFTs that others can enjoy or trade.

Supporting Charity: A portion of every transaction goes to mental health initiatives, making every laugh count.
